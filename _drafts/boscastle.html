Boscastle Road

Dartmouth Park

Boscastle Road

Contract Value: £200,000
Year: 2015

Our Client had lived in this terraced house for several years and had undertaken some improvements to it when purchased.

With their family growing, however, they decided to complete and upgrade the house; our project involved a high grade kitchen, new hardwood flooring, remodelling of the 1st floor including 2 new bathrooms, playroom and laundry as well as new study areas, joinery, bookcases, dressers etc.
