We obtained planning, building regulation and party wall permissions for an extensive rear extensions as well as considerable structural work to open up the new extension to the main reception rooms of the house to form an enormous open plan living, eating and cooking area. Upstairs we reconfigured the middle floor into 4 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms and the top floor into a huge ensuite master bedroom with terrace and walk in dressing room.

Throughout the house we built extensive joinery, bookshelves, wardrobes, storage as well as complete rewiring, plumbing, heating and AV including two TV systems.
