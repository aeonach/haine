We obtained planning permission for a fully glazed rear extension – supporting steelwork allowed the main reception rooms to be knocked together, open plan to the new extension.

A new kitchen was commissioned, two new bathrooms created, extensive rewiring and heating alterations were completed as well as new joinery and wardrobes.
