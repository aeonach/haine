Complex party wall negotiations were needed as was planning and building regulation permissions before a full competitive tender process was completed. A specialist basement builder was employed who was retained on completion of the dig to carry out the fitting out of the new space into a bedroom suite, additional shower room/cloakroom, large playroom, storage plus a considerable new area of cupboarding, wine storage etc.
